Three tiers. All honest pricing, no hidden fees.

## The plans

**Bronze.** Free. You get the booking page, services, calendar, and basic intake. The intelligence layer (body map, AI features, longitudinal patterns) is limited to the first five sessions per client. After that, those features lock until you upgrade.

**Silver.** Nineteen dollars a month, or one hundred ninety dollars a year. Full intelligence layer for unlimited sessions. Cancellation policy with auto-charging. Card-on-file. Refunds from your dashboard. Lapsed-client outreach. Most therapists are on Silver.

**Gold.** Forty-nine dollars a month, or four hundred ninety dollars a year. Everything in Silver, plus advanced AI features, priority email support, and a white-label booking page (your domain, no MyBodyMap branding).

## Founding therapist program

The first one hundred therapists get Silver tier free for life. No credit card required at signup. To receive a Silver-free-for-life coupon, message us on Instagram (mybodymap01) or Facebook. We will send the code BETAONE which gives you Silver at one hundred percent off for twelve months. After that, you stay on Silver permanently as part of the founding cohort.

## What we do not charge for

Things some competitors charge for that we do not:
- Card-on-file capture (free on every plan including Bronze)
- Cancellation policy with auto-charge (Silver and Gold)
- Online deposits at booking
- One-tap refunds
- Email broadcasts to your client list

## Stripe and Square fees

Whatever processor you connect (Stripe or Square or both) charges their own fees on each transaction, typically 2.9 percent plus thirty cents for cards. We do not take a cut. The processor's fee goes to the processor, the rest goes to your bank.

## When prices may change

We grandfather every existing customer at their current rate, indefinitely. If we ever raise prices, only new signups pay the new amount. Founding therapists stay on Silver-free forever.
