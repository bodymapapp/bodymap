Your existing client list moves with you. We can import a CSV from any major platform.

## The export step (from your old platform)

Most platforms let you export clients as a CSV file:

- **Vagaro:** Settings, then Reports, then Customer Export
- **MassageBook:** Reports, then Client List, then Export
- **Acuity:** Clients, then the Export button at the top
- **GlossGenius:** Settings, Reports, Client Export
- **Square Appointments:** Customers, More menu, Export
- **Paper or Excel:** save your spreadsheet as CSV

If your platform makes export hard, screenshot a few rows of your client spreadsheet and send it to hello@mybodymap.app. We will help.

## What columns we accept

Required: name, email or phone (one or both)
Optional but helpful: last visit date, address, notes, birthday, total sessions, total revenue

If your CSV has different column names than ours, the import tool lets you map them on screen. No editing the file.

## The import step (in MyBodyMap)

1. In your dashboard, go to Clients
2. Click Import Clients (top right)
3. Upload the CSV
4. Map the columns (the tool guesses most of them)
5. Click Import

Most lists of one hundred to five hundred clients import in under a minute.

## What happens after import

- Your clients get added to your client list, marked as imported
- They do NOT get an email automatically (we never message your clients without your explicit go-ahead)
- Their previous session history (if you included it in the CSV) shows in their profile
- They can book through your new MyBodyMap booking page once you share it

## Sending a "we moved" email to imported clients

After importing, you can send a one-time announcement email to your full client list. Settings, then Email, then send to all. We have a template ready. Or you can write your own. Either way, signed by you, in your voice, sent from your domain.
