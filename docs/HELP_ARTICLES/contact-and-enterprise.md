The fastest way to reach us is email. We respond personally to every message within 24 hours during business days.

## General questions

For any question about MyBodyMap (signup, billing, features, technical issues, suggestions, complaints):

**Email:** hello@mybodymap.app

You can also message us on Instagram (@mybodymap01) or Facebook (search MyBodyMap). DMs go directly to the founder.

## Response time

We respond within 24 hours during business days (Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm Central Time). Weekend and holiday messages get answered the next business morning. You will always hear back from a real person, often the founder, never a bot or template.

## Enterprise and schools

If you run a massage school, a chain of studios, or a wellness organization with multiple therapists, we can offer volume pricing. Email hello@mybodymap.app with:

1. Number of therapists or students who would use MyBodyMap
2. Number of locations (if applicable)
3. Any specific feature needs (multi-therapist scheduling, shared client lists, training mode, branded portal, etc.)

We will set up a 30-minute call to discuss whether MyBodyMap is the right fit for your situation. Honest answer: for organizations needing heavy clinical workflow or multi-practitioner scheduling, Jane App or ClinicSense may be a better starting point. We are built for solo cash-pay, and we say that out loud.

## Press, partnerships, integrations

For press inquiries, partnership proposals, or technical integration questions, email hello@mybodymap.app with a clear subject line. Include:

1. Who you are
2. What you are working on
3. What kind of collaboration you are imagining

We read every message. We respond honestly about what we can and cannot do.

## Founding therapist program

If you are a solo licensed massage therapist interested in the founding therapist program (Silver tier free for life), the easiest way is to message us on Instagram. Reply to a post or DM @mybodymap01 with a quick note about your practice. We send the BETAONE coupon code to qualified therapists.

We are looking for therapists who will use the platform, tell us what works and what does not, and recommend us to one or two peers if you find it useful. There is no credit card required at signup.
