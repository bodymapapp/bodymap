Signing up takes about thirty seconds. Setting up your practice afterward takes about thirty minutes. No credit card required.

## The signup flow

1. Visit mybodymap.app and click Start free
2. Enter your email and a password
3. Pick a custom URL, the part that goes after mybodymap.app/. Most therapists pick their first name or business name. Examples: mybodymap.app/healinghands, mybodymap.app/sarah, mybodymap.app/touchstonewellness
4. Confirm your email by clicking the link we send you
5. Land in your dashboard

That is it. You are signed up.

## What to do next

After signup, three things matter most for getting going:

1. **Add your services.** Go to Settings, then Services. Add the work you actually do (deep tissue 60 min, prenatal 90 min, etc.) with prices and durations.
2. **Set your hours.** Settings, then Availability. Tell us when you work and when you don't.
3. **Connect a payment processor.** Settings, then Payments. Pick Stripe or Square. Whichever you already use is fine. You can add the other later.

Now your booking page is live. Share the link with your clients.

## Founding therapist program

The first one hundred therapists get Silver tier free for life. No credit card. We are looking for therapists who will use the platform, tell us what works and what does not, and recommend us to one or two peers if you find it useful.

If you want a Silver-tier coupon, message us on Instagram (mybodymap01) or Facebook. We will send the code.
