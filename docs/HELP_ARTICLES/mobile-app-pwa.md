MyBodyMap works as a Progressive Web App (PWA). You install it from your phone's browser to your home screen. From there it behaves like any native app: full screen, no browser bar, fast launch, push notifications.

## Why a PWA instead of a native app

Three reasons:

1. **One codebase, every device.** iPhone, Android, iPad, desktop browser, all the same app. Updates roll out everywhere at once. No App Store delays.
2. **No App Store gatekeeping.** Apple and Google can take 30 percent of certain transactions through their stores. We pass that savings to you by not being there.
3. **Instant updates.** When we ship a fix, you get it on next launch. No "please update the app" prompts.

The tradeoff: the install step is one tap less convenient than the App Store. We lead you through it on your first mobile visit.

## How to install on iPhone (Safari)

1. Open mybodymap.app in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow up)
3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
4. Tap Add

A MyBodyMap icon appears on your home screen. Tap it to launch.

## How to install on Android (Chrome)

1. Open mybodymap.app in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
3. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen
4. Tap Install

A MyBodyMap icon appears on your home screen. Tap it to launch.

## What the installed app gives you

- Full screen, no browser address bar
- Faster launch (cached locally)
- Push notifications when a client books or cancels
- Works offline for read-only views (your schedule and client list still load even on a flaky connection)

## Push notifications

Settings, then Notifications. You can choose what triggers a push:

- New booking received
- Client cancelled
- Cancellation policy charge fired
- Lapsed client outreach scheduled
- Daily morning brief

If you do not want any push notifications, leave them all off. Email backup notifications still work.

## Offline mode

If you lose internet during a session (basement studio, rural visit), the installed app keeps showing you:
- Today's schedule (read-only)
- Client profiles (read-only)
- Past session history (read-only)

Bookings, intake form submissions, and payments require internet. Once you reconnect, everything syncs automatically.
