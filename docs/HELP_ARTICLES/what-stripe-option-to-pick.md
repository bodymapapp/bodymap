Short answer: if you already use Stripe for any reason, tap **"Link your Stripe account"** (the purple button). It takes 15 seconds. If you have never used Stripe, tap the smaller link below it to **"Set up a new Stripe account"** which walks you through creating one.

## The two paths, explained

When you tap Connect Stripe in Settings, you see two options. They both end up with payments working through MyBodyMap. The difference is which Stripe account is doing the work.

### Link your Stripe account (recommended for most therapists)

This is for therapists who already have a Stripe account from any past business activity, including:

- A different practice you ran before
- A side business or e-commerce store
- A previous platform like Squarespace that set one up for you
- Selling on Etsy, Shopify, or anywhere else Stripe powers payments
- Even a trial Stripe account you set up once and never used

You tap the button, Stripe asks you to sign in (you might already be signed in), Stripe asks if MyBodyMap can use your account on your behalf, you tap Allow. Done. We get permission to process payments using your existing account.

### Set up a new Stripe account (for therapists new to Stripe)

This is for therapists who have never used Stripe. The flow asks you for your business name, bank account, identity verification, and a few business details. Takes about five to ten minutes. You end up with a brand new Stripe account that lives partly inside our platform.

## Why does this difference matter?

If you already have a Stripe account and we make you create a NEW one through us, you lose a few things:

- Your existing customer relationships in Stripe (saved cards on file from your previous business, customer profiles, transaction history)
- Continuity of your tax reporting. You will get two separate 1099-K forms at tax time instead of one
- The accounts are operationally separate. Funds from MyBodyMap go to the new account; funds from your other business go to the old one. Two bank reconciliations instead of one.

By linking your existing account, all of that stays together.

## What if I do not know whether I have a Stripe account?

If you have ever sold anything online with credit cards in the last few years, you probably do. Try the recommended path first. When you tap Link your Stripe account, Stripe will ask you to sign in. If you have an account, your saved login or email will work. If you do not, Stripe will tell you and you can fall back to setting up a new one.

## Is one safer than the other?

No. Both use the same Stripe infrastructure. Stripe handles all the card data, PCI compliance, fraud detection, dispute resolution. Your data is equally secure either way.

## Can I switch later?

Yes but it is not one-click. If you connect with one option and want to switch to the other, contact us at hello@mybodymap.app. We will help you transition without losing data.

## What if Candice or someone else helped me set this up before?

If you already see Stripe as Connected in Settings (green panel), you are good. Nothing to change.
