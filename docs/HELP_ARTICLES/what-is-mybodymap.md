MyBodyMap is practice management software made for solo licensed massage therapists. We do the work that does not require your hands. Booking, intake forms, reminders, session notes, lapsed-client outreach, payments, cancellation policies. All of it runs automatically so you can spend your time on the table with your clients, not in front of a laptop.

We are not for spas or chains. We are not for therapists who bill insurance heavily. We are for solo cash-pay practitioners who want their evenings back.

## Who built it

Built by a solo founder, self-funded, no investor pressure. We grow only as fast as our customers tell us we should. Our long-term plan is to be a sustainable small business that serves therapists for decades, not to chase a unicorn outcome.

## What makes it different

Three things competitors do not do well:

1. **Body-map intelligence.** Your clients tell you where they hurt by tapping a body diagram. Over time we build a heatmap that shows you patterns no other software captures.
2. **Retention-first design.** Every feature is built to bring the client back, not just to take the booking.
3. **No payment lock-in.** Use Stripe or Square or both. Most competitors force you onto their own merchant relationship.

## Where to start

Sign up at mybodymap.app. Setup takes about thirty minutes, most of which is you setting your own services and prices.
