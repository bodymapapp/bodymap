Intake forms are how new clients tell you about their body, their history, and what they need. MyBodyMap intake is built specifically for massage therapy. The body map is the part competitors do not have.

## What clients see at first booking

When someone books with you for the first time, before the session, they get an email with a link to fill out the intake. The link opens a mobile-friendly form with these sections:

1. **Basic info.** Name, contact, emergency contact, birthday.
2. **Body map.** A diagram of the human body. They tap the areas where they have pain, tension, or focus needs. They can tap multiple areas. For each tapped area, they can add a note.
3. **Health history.** Recent injuries, ongoing conditions, medications, allergies, surgeries.
4. **Session preferences.** Pressure (light, medium, deep), music preference, talking preference, room temperature, oils and aromatherapy.
5. **Waiver.** Standard liability waiver they sign electronically.

Most clients complete the intake in under five minutes on their phone.

## What you see when they arrive

In your dashboard, two minutes before each session, the pre-session brief shows:

- The body map (with their tapped areas highlighted)
- Any health flags (ongoing conditions, allergies)
- Their preferences from the form
- A two to three sentence AI summary of what to focus on

You read it in thirty seconds before they walk in. They feel like you have been thinking about them all week.

## The body map in detail

This is the differentiator. The body map captures structured data, not free text. Over time, MyBodyMap shows you patterns:

- Right shoulder marked in 70 percent of this client's intakes
- Lower back tension correlates with this client's stressful weeks at work
- Left calf has been a recurring focus for eight months

Competitors capture this in unstructured text notes that you can never query later. We capture it as data you can search, sort, and visualize across all clients.

## Customizing the intake

Settings, then Intake Forms. You can:

- Add custom questions (any text or multiple choice)
- Remove questions you do not need
- Reorder sections
- Mark questions as required or optional
- Add a separate intake for specific service types (e.g. prenatal intake versus standard)

Default intake works for most therapists. Customization is optional.

## Returning clients

When a client books their second session, they do not redo the entire intake. Smart pre-fill loads their last intake, marks it "as of last visit," and asks only:

- Has anything changed in your health?
- Do you want to update preferences?
- What would you like to focus on this session?

Most clients hit Confirm without changing anything. Five seconds versus five minutes.

## Where the data lives

Each client's intake history is in their profile. Click any past session, see the intake exactly as it was that day. You can compare intake-over-time to see how their body has changed.
