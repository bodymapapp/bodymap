A fair question. Worth answering directly.

## Your data is yours, always exportable

Settings, Export Data. Three buttons:

- **Export clients** (CSV file with all client info, including contact, history, notes)
- **Export sessions** (CSV file with every session, dates, services, prices)
- **Export everything** (a zip file with the above, plus intake forms, billing history, settings)

You can do this any time, no questions asked. Run it monthly if you want. We never block your data behind a paywall or charge for export.

## Why we are unlikely to disappear suddenly

Three structural reasons:

1. **Self-funded by the founder.** No investor pressure to grow at any cost or pivot suddenly. We grow only as our customers tell us we should.
2. **Profitable on every paying customer.** The economics work without venture capital. We do not have a runway clock.
3. **HK has a documented succession plan.** If the founder is unable to continue, the Founder Runbook describes how a small team can take over operations cleanly. The handoff is rehearsed in advance.

## The most likely failure modes (honestly)

If MyBodyMap ever shut down, here is how it would go:

1. **Ninety days notice.** You get a clear email at least 90 days before any shutdown. Plenty of time to migrate.
2. **A guided export process.** We help you move to a competitor with the least friction possible.
3. **Refunds for unused subscription time.** If you paid annually, the unused months get refunded.
4. **Client data preserved.** Your data stays accessible for at least 90 days after shutdown so you can continue exporting.

We would never ghost our customers. The whole product philosophy is built on respect for therapists.

## Our backup of your data

In addition to the regular database backups (which we keep), the Founder Hub has an internal "download all docs" mechanism that stores a complete snapshot of internal documentation. Not your client data, but the documentation that lets a successor team understand the platform.

## Migration to other platforms

Your CSV exports work directly with:

- Vagaro (Customer Import)
- MassageBook (Client Import)
- Acuity (Customer CSV)
- Jane App (Patient Import)
- GlossGenius (Client Import)
- Square Appointments (Customer Import)

We will not put barriers in your way. Your client relationships are your business, not ours.

## What you can do as a precaution

If you want to feel completely safe:

1. Run Export Data once a month
2. Save the file to your own backup (Dropbox, Google Drive, an external drive)
3. Keep a copy of the email addresses of your top 20 clients somewhere outside MyBodyMap

This is good practice with any software. We just make it easy.

## The honest bottom line

Software companies do disappear. Even big ones. Anyone who tells you "we will never go out of business" is lying. What you can ask for is: clear ownership of your data, easy export, advance notice if anything changes. We commit to all three.
