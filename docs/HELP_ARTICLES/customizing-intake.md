The default intake works for most therapists. Customize it when you have a specific need that the defaults do not cover.

## Where to customize

Settings, then Intake Forms. You will see the default intake displayed as a list of sections, each with a list of questions.

For each question you can:
- Edit the wording
- Mark required or optional
- Remove the question entirely
- Move it to a different section

To add a new question, scroll to the end of any section and click Add question. Pick the type (short text, long text, single choice, multiple choice, yes/no), write the question, and save.

## Common customizations

**Adding a "How did you hear about me?" question.** Useful for tracking which marketing efforts work. Multiple choice: Instagram, Google, Friend referral, Walk-in, Other.

**Adding a "What is your goal for this session?" question.** Long text. Helps you focus the session on what they want most.

**Adding industry-specific questions.** Prenatal therapists add questions about pregnancy stage, prior births, comfort positions. Sports therapists add questions about training schedule, recent competitions, injury history.

**Removing the emergency contact field.** Some therapists prefer to keep this conversation in person. Toggle the field to hidden.

## Service-specific intakes

You can create different intake forms for different services. Settings, Intake Forms, then New variant.

Common variants:
- Standard intake (Swedish, deep tissue)
- Prenatal intake
- Sports rehabilitation intake
- First-time only versus returning

When a client books a service, the intake form they see is the one tied to that service. You set which intake applies to which service in the service editor.

## What you cannot remove

Two things stay in every intake form by design:

1. **The body map.** This is the data backbone of MyBodyMap. Every other feature (longitudinal heatmaps, pattern detection, pre-session brief) depends on it.
2. **The waiver.** Legal protection for you. Required.

You can edit the waiver text to match your local requirements (states have different rules) but you cannot remove the signature step.

## Saving and previewing

After making changes, click Preview to see exactly what a client will see. Open it on your phone to verify the mobile experience.

Changes apply to NEW intakes only. Existing client profiles are not retroactively rewritten when you change the form. Their history is preserved as-is at the time they filled it.

## When to leave the defaults alone

If you are new to MyBodyMap, run with the defaults for at least one month. The defaults were chosen based on what most therapists actually use. Customize after you have seen the data flow through a few sessions and noticed what is missing.
