## Critical rules to honor (from the playbook)

- **No new data asks.** Every formula uses data we already have, or seeded with sensible defaults.
- **Defaults work for 95% of therapists out of the box.** Settings overrides exist but are never required.
- **No AI-generated prose without a deterministic backbone.** Regex fallback ships first. AI layered on top.
- **Familiar surfaces, proprietary intelligence on top.** Calendar still looks like a calendar. Intelligence is the moat.
- **Never use "AI-powered" in customer copy.** Say "your platform notices..." instead.
